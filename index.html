Doctoral Researcher

October 2020 - January 2021

Cranfield University, Integrated Vehicle Health Management (IVHM) Centre

Research focus: Platform Aircraft Health Management Using AI

This research explores an approach to Aircraft Health Management by utilizing sensor data collected at the platform level. It involves developing a "virtual aircraft" by integrating the Digital Twins of various aircraft systems in MATLAB to simulate flight over a defined flight profile, enabling data collection across the entire flight envelope. The collected data is then processed to design a classifier for identifying faults and their severities, along with the development of a health index to effectively monitor the behaviour of fault modes. This approach promises supply to maintenance information for aircraft health management without directly using the diagnostics of the aircraft systems.
